About WordInspo:

WordInspo can help you improve your writing!

First: Enter a keyword.

Second: Select whether you would like to receive the synonyms or antonyms of that word.

Third: Click "Search!" WordInspo will display the keyword's definition, and a synonym or antonym word cloud!
